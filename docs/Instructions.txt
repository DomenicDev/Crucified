INSTRUCTIONS

PLAYING AS MONSTER

Your goal is to kill the human player. 
The demon can throw fireballs at the player to hurt and eventually kill him.
The demon has to kill the player before all artifacts have been destroyed.
The artifacts are not visible to the demon.


PLAYING THE SURVIVOR

The goal of the human player is to find and destroy all artifacts.
The human player has to throw the artifacts into the containers (old stone ruins).
Those containers can be found all around the map.
Only one artifact can be thrown into one ruins container.
Artifacts can be found all around the map.



DEMON CONTROLS

Left click - Throwing fireball


HUMAN CONTROLS

F Key - Pick up items and throwing artifacts into container
